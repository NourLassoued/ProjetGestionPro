Features

Everything you need to manage your projects

Powerful tools designed to help teams work smarter.

Kanban Boards

Drag & drop tasks between To Do, In Progress, Done. Visual workflow for everyone.

Team Chat

Real-time messaging per project. Discuss, share updates, and decide together.

Dashboard

Track progress with visual stats — completion rates, task distribution, progress bars.

Role Management

Admin, Member, Observer — assign roles and control access. Secure and flexible.

Smart Cards

Each card tracks title, description, deadline, status, and auto-detects the author.

Tables & Projects

Hierarchical structure: Tables → Projects → Cards. Perfect for complex workflows.
